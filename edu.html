Education

I was born and brought up in Karachi, Pakistan. After completing my secondary schooling, I got admission to Karachi Grammar School, one of the most prestigious high schools in my city. From here, I completed my GCE A'Levels and graduated with a distinction across all my core subjects - Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Economics.

Tertiary Education


The University of Hong Kong

Based on my outstanding A'Levels results, I was awarded a full scholarship to The University of Hong Kong (HKU). I majored in Mechanical Engineering with coursework in a variety of disciplines, including:

  • Energy Conversion Systems
  • Building Service Engineering
  • Materials for Engineering Applications
  • Aeronautical Engineering

During my four years at HKU, I also served as a Student Ambassador. This was a unique experience through which I got a chance to represent HKU at home and abroad. By speaking at university fairs, freshmen orientations, and conducting vistor tours, I was able to hone my interpersonal skills.
